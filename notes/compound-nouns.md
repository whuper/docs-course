#### 比较orient 和 object

orient,及物动词
magazines oriented to the business community (省略了which is)
面向商界的杂志

Object,作不及物动词时
to object to [something]/[somebody];
反对某事/某人
she objects to his drinking
她反对他喝酒


oriented to :面向
